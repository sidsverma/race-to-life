Race to Life

The Journey of Life

Welcome to Race to Life, where you'll experience one of nature's most incredible journeys!

Control a sperm cell competing against millions of others in a race through obstacles to reach and fertilize the egg.

Your mission:

  • Navigate through natural barriers in the reproductive system
  • Compete against other cells for survival
  • Collect energy boosts for speed and vitality
  • Use power-ups to increase your chances

Only the strongest and most determined will succeed in this celebration of life's beginnings!
